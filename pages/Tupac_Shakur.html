Tupac Amaru Shakur ( / ˈ t uː p ɑː k ʃ ə ˈ k ʊər / TOO -pahk shə- KOOR ) [2] (born Lesane Parish Crooks ; June 16, 1971 – September 13, 1996), also known by his stage names 2Pac and Makaveli , was an American rapper, poet and actor. [3] Shakur sold over 75 million records worldwide, making him one of the best-selling music artists of all time. [4] His double disc albums All Eyez on Me (1996) and his Greatest Hits (1998) are among the best-selling albums in the United States . [5] Shakur is consistently ranked as one of the greatest and most influential rappers of all time [6] and he has been listed and ranked as one of the greatest artists of any genre by many publications, including Rolling Stone , which ranked him 86th on its list of The 100 Greatest Artists of All Time . [7] On April 7, 2017, Shakur was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in his first year of eligibility. [8]
